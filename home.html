Our Delicacies

Matooke

Mashed and served with a curry of your choice

Price: $2.5
A staple food in Central Uganda. Our matooke is steamed on low heat, and mashed to a soft, starchy consistency. A spicy curry or groundnut sauce always compliment matooke. The flavor is in the sauce. This meal comes with vegetables

Category: Lunch

Rolex

Chapatti, 2 eggs, shredded cheese, minced chillies, tomatoes, onions and cabbage

Regular: $5 Large: $8
A popular snack on the streets of Kampala. The rolex is a vegetable omelet rolled up in a chapati, its name a cheeky reference to the watch brand. Minced chiles add a bit of heat, with fresh chopped cilantro as a garnish.

Category: Breakfast

Luwombo

Choice of Groundnuts, Lamb, Beef or Chicken

  1. Chicken Luwombo: $9.8
  2. Beef/Lamb Luwombo: $10.5
  3. Groundnuts Luwombo: $7.6
We prepare Luwombo the way it has been made for generations in Buganda. We use only the freshest ingredients and slow cooking, allowing the flavors to meld together and create a truly unforgettable taste and aroma.

Category: Lunch

Pilau

Cooked with your choice of meat and spiced with fresh spices

  1. Chicken Pilau: $9.9
  2. Beef/Lamb Pilau: $8.5
Pilau has a wonderful balance of flavors. This festive dish, is made with rice cooked in a well-seasoned broth of Meat or chicken. Unlike the Indian pilau, the East African version does not use curry and is less spicy. Not forgetting pilau masala

Category: Lunch
